About Us

WhiteStone Capital is fictional organization in HackClub's Fictional Republic. WhiteStone is a purely capitalist organization that focuses on maximizing profits while maintaining a strong ethical foundation. Our mission is to make Democratic Republic of HackClub the richest place on Earth while keeping a slice of the pie for ourselves.

Our Values

  • Integrity in all our dealings
  • Commitment to excellence
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Community engagement and support

Our Founder

WhiteStone Capital was founded by Krish Gupta, a visionary leader with a passion for entrepreneurship and a commitment to making a positive impact in the world. With a background in finance and technology, Krish has built WhiteStone Capital into a leading organization in the fictional economy of HackClub.
